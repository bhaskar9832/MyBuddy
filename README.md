# MyBuddy 🤖

> Automate Your Monthly Tasks With a Single Click

MyBuddy is an intelligent automation platform that simplifies your monthly routine tasks. From utility bill payments to service bookings, let MyBuddy handle the repetitive work so you can focus on what matters.

## 🎯 Features

- **Automatic Bill Payments** - Pay electricity, gas, and WiFi bills on schedule without manual intervention
- **Service Bookings** - Automatic gas cylinder bookings and other monthly service requests
- **Smart Autopay** - Set up automatic payments once, and let the app handle recurring charges
- **One-Click Setup** - Simple permission-based authorization - no complex configurations
- **Recurring Automation** - Schedule and automate any monthly task
- **Payment Integration** - Secure integration with multiple payment gateways

## 🚀 How It Works

1. **Allow Permissions** - Grant MyBuddy permission to access your accounts and payment methods
2. **Set Automations** - Choose which bills and services you want to automate
3. **Sit Back & Relax** - MyBuddy handles everything automatically each month
4. **Track Everything** - View detailed logs and notifications for all automated transactions

## 📋 Supported Services

- **Utilities**
  - Electricity Bill Payments
  - Gas Bill Payments
  - WiFi/Internet Bill Payments
  
- **Services**
  - Gas Cylinder Bookings
  - Water Supply Payments
  - Other Monthly Subscriptions
